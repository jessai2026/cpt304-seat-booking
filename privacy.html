Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What data we collect

This application stores data only in your browser's localStorage. No personal data is transmitted to any server. The data stored includes your booking preferences (selected seats, service settings) and your cookie consent choice.

2. Why we use localStorage

localStorage allows the application to remember your seat selections and service settings between visits without requiring a user account or sending data to a remote server.

3. Cookie consent

When you first visit this application, a banner asks for your consent to use localStorage. You may accept or decline. If you decline, no data will be saved and your preferences will be lost when you close the browser.

4. Third-party services

This application is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) as part of their hosting service. Please refer to Vercel's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (DevTools → Application → Local Storage → Clear).

6. Contact

This application was created as part of a university coursework project. For any questions, please contact the development team.
